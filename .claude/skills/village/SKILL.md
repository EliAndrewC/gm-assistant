# Villages and Rural Communities

Generate farming villages, hamlets, village headsmen, and rural life details consistent with the established demographics and social structure.

## Source Material

This skill draws primarily from reference files rather than containing its own large source block. The key sources are:

- `/setting/village-headsmen.md` - The definitive reference on village headsman role, selection, compensation, loyalty, land management, and dispute resolution
- `/setting/median-domain.md` - Population distribution: ~216 villages and ~1296 hamlets per domain
- `/setting/demographics.md` - Detailed breakdowns of hamlet/village populations by caste and occupation
- `/setting/economics.md` - Rent/tax system, merchant landlords, farm plot economics
- `/setting/professions.md` - Ashigaru (armed peasant militia), country monks, entertainers

When generating a village, consult these files for the established numbers, social dynamics, and economic realities.
