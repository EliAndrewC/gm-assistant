# Temple Relics

The GM source material for temple relics — the Temple of Bishamon's relics, cursed items (Armor of Fool's Regret, Candle of Tears), the Yamaoroshi sword backstory, and the broader essay on supernatural ambiguity — has its canonical home in [`/.claude/skills/relic/SKILL.md`](../.claude/skills/relic/SKILL.md).

Per project convention (see [`/workspace/CLAUDE.md`](../CLAUDE.md) → "Single Canonical Home for Source Blocks"), this file is retained as a campaign-side discoverability pointer and does not duplicate the source block.

## What lives in /relic/SKILL.md

- The Temple of Bishamon's relics in Toshi Ranbo: the Staff of Bishamon, the Pillar of Ten Thousand Names, the Prayer Beads of the Guardian Spirits, the Sword of the First Vow, the Helm of Discerning Valor, the Eternal Wisdom Tree, and the Chamber of Celestial Echoes
- The stolen relics taken by the Tsume during their occupation: the Tooth of the Shinsei, the Jade Shinsei, the Chime of Harmony
- The GM's worked examples of supernatural ambiguity — the Yamaoroshi sword, the Armor of Fool's Regret, the Candle of Tears
- The Toyohiro / Mirai / Korihime stories illustrating the ambiguity-of-supernatural-experience theme
- Spirit-encounter mechanics (the five-rings system, connection damage) used in dream-quest scenes
