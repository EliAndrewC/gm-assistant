# Cosmology Reference

Religion, mythology, and supernatural elements of Rokugan. Referenced by `/fortune`, `/temple`, `/vow`, and `/moto` skills, and relevant whenever generating content involving the gods, spirits, magic, or religious institutions.

## Files

- [moon-court.md](moon-court.md) - Lord Moon's heavenly court: Ryoshun (Guardian), Nanube (Deceiver), Goju (Hunter), Suro (Thief). Their origins and domains.
- [moon-stories.md](moon-stories.md) - Mythological stories: Saibankan's ruling (Lord Moon and Lady Sun shaping humanity), Nanube's "No Answer to Believe", Goju's "Shattered Names", Suro's "Balance Exploited", "A Fishy Conversation"
- [maho.md](maho.md) - Maho (blood magic) and bloodspeakers: 7 disciplines (Shadowlands taint, Name magic, Wound magic, Summoning, Crafting, Dark Divination, Fleshshaping). Coven structure, detection.
- [burning-sands.md](burning-sands.md) - Burning Sands Desert, gaijin creation fables, blessings and curses
- [fortunes.md](fortunes.md) - The 7 Major Fortunes, Kitsu Okura's 6 doctrines on why Fortunes don't give clearer guidance, Doji Shizue play scene. Key reference for `/fortune` skill.
- [between-places.md](between-places.md) - Locations coexisting between realms (mortal world/Yomi/etc), mechanical effects of supernatural contact
