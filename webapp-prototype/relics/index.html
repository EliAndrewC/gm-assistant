Relics

A working pool of sacred objects, drawn from across the Empire. Each tied to a named hand; each kept by a Custodian. Choose one for your story, or generate another.

Forty-two relics across seven fortunes. Press a seal above to focus a single fortune; the rest will fall away.

The pool is a starting point. Every entry can be placed in any plausible temple of its Fortune - within the clan's lands if a clan is named, anywhere in the Empire if marked any.
